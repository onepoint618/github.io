About Me

Welcome to my portfolio

As an experienced business analyst, I am passionate about creating and delivering solutions which exceed expectations. Using an agile mindset and a design thinking approach, I help to ensure stakeholders achieve high value outcomes.

My cross-industry IT background includes roles encompassing new product development, project delivery and operational improvement initiatives.

I am highly regarded for ability to act as the glue between business and IT stakeholders, with the business acumen to understand needs and translate to technical requirements

Beyond professional life, you’ll find me spending time with family & friends, being outside, fixing or building things, playing with tech (especially Raspberry Pi projects), listening to a podcast or music.

Mind The Gap

I was engaged as a business analyst for a large-scale infrastructure transformation project for a European public sector client who was starting their cloud journey. I was tasked with validating and remedying a number of significant operational gaps in the private cloud solution being deployed. The scope encompassed the full end-to-end server lifecycle, from initial request to decommissioning.

I facilitated the cross-functional workshops, engaging the stakeholders to reach agreement and buy-in. The scope included current/ future state mapping of IT operational processes, process definition, just-enough documentation and role allocation. Overall service delivery quality and performance was significantly improved and confusion on responsibilities was resolved.

Test Me

I was engaged on a private cloud implementation project for a leading Australian tertiary education provider; The requirements for integration and user acceptance testing (UAT) came into scope late, resulting in a project risk which needed to be mitigated.

I developed the overall Test Strategy, Test Plan, & Test Cases, and ensured that all stakeholders were aligned and in agreement. I successfully onboarded and coached Test Manager and Test Team, ensuring a seamless deployment for the customer. I standardized the approach and "templatised" the testing artefacts for future cloud deployment projects. The customer also used the tests as a basis for their own user testing and acceptance.
