About Me

Welcome to my portfolio

As an experienced business analyst, I am passionate about creating and delivering solutions which exceed expectations. Using an agile mindset and a design thinking approach, I help to ensure stakeholders achieve high value outcomes.

I leverage my curiosity and strong consultative skills to excel in requirements elicitation & gathering, cross-team liaison, effective stakeholder engagement and communication.

My cross-industry IT technical background includes roles encompassing product development, project delivery and operational improvement initiatives.

I am highly regarded for ability to act as the glue between business and IT stakeholders, with the business acumen to understand needs and translate to technical requirements

When I am not at work, you’ll find me spending time with family & friends, being outside, fixing or building things, playing with tech (eg RPi), listening to a podcast or music.
